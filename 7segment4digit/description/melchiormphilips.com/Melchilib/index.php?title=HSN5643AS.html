4-Digit 0.56" 7-Segment Red LED Display

The HSN5643AS is a 4-Digit 0.56" 7-Segment Red LED Display with Common Anode. It can be purchased from Chinese wholesale companies.

Pins

The component has a common Anode, so the pins work by activating all segments you want lit, and selecting the digit you want as the ground.

Pin Name Use
1 E Powers E (lower left) segment on the selected digit
2 D Powers D (bottom) segment on the selected digit
3 H Powers H (dot) segment on the selected digit
4 C Powers C (lower right) segment on the selected digit
5 G Powers G (middle) segment on the selected digit
6 DSP4 Selects the 4th Digit
7 DSP1 Selects the 1st Digit
8 A Powers A (top) segment on the selected digit
9 F Powers F (upper left) segment on the selected digit
10 DSP2 Selects the 2nd Digit
11 DSP3 Selects the 3rd Digit
12 B Powers segment B (upper right) segment on the selected digit
